Wrapped with Love

A bouquet of love,
sent across
the miles...
No expectations,
no demands...
just pure love from
my heart to yours.
Every flower
represents a silent
prayer I make for
your happiness.
Two years of loving
you quietly, deeply,
and
unconditionally.
Even under a
different sky,
my heart beats
only for you.
Your smile is
my favorite view,
even from a
distance.
Far apart in
distance, but always
close in my
thoughts.
Loving you from
afar is my heart’s
most beautiful
secret.
